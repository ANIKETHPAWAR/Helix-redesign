Who we are

Founded in 2011, Helix Infrastructure Consultants Pvt. Ltd. brings together specialists across civil engineering, real estate consulting, financial structuring and project marketing. We operate as an extended team for land owners, builders, institutions and investors who seek predictable, risk-managed outcomes.

Our work spans educational campuses, residential communities, commercial spaces and institutional infrastructure. We are comfortable stepping in at any stage – from early feasibility through to post-handover sales – but our best work happens when we are engaged end-to-end.

Vision

To be the most trusted infrastructure and real estate consulting partner for organisations, land owners and investors across India – known for our integrity, technical depth and execution discipline.

Mission

To consistently deliver infrastructure assets that are financially viable, technically sound and operationally resilient, while protecting the long-term interests of our clients.
