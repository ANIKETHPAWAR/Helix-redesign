What this service covers

Helix helps clients make informed decisions about when, where and how to transact in real estate – whether you are buying, selling or structuring development collaborations.

We operate as your independent advisor, combining on-ground market knowledge with financial and technical insights so that your decisions are de-risked and aligned with your long-term goals.

For land owners & HNIs

  • ✓ Best-use options for land and built assets.
  • ✓ Joint development and revenue-sharing structuring.
  • ✓ Transparent evaluation of proposals from developers.

Who it is for

Land owners, investors & institutions

Real Estate Consulting is relevant if you:

  • ✓ Want to maximise value from land or existing properties.
  • ✓ Need an advisor to evaluate new investments or exits.
  • ✓ Require portfolio-level guidance for family offices or institutions.
