What this service covers

Helix’s Project Management & Turnkey offering is designed for stakeholders who want a single, accountable partner to coordinate all moving parts of an infrastructure or real estate project.

We align consultants, contractors, vendors, lenders and marketing partners to a shared plan – ensuring that technical quality, timelines and commercial outcomes are all managed cohesively.

Turnkey vs. management-only models

Depending on your internal capabilities and risk appetite, Helix can either:

  • 1 Work as a pure project management partner alongside your chosen vendors; or
  • 2 Structure a turnkey engagement where Helix manages a larger share of scopes under an integrated contract.

Who it is for

Owners, developers & institutions

This service is ideal if you:

  • ✓ Lack the capacity to manage multiple vendors and consultants internally.
  • ✓ Need strong timeline and budget control for time-sensitive projects.
  • ✓ Want a structured governance layer between promoters and on-ground teams.

Service breakdown

Integrated planning, execution and market-aligned delivery.

We plug into all key stages of your project, with clear responsibilities defined at each step.

01 · Program setup

Definition & governance
