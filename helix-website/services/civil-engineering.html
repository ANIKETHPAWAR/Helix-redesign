Civil Engineering & Construction.

Home / Services / Civil Engineering & Construction

What this service covers

Helix acts as a technical partner for organisations, institutions, land owners and developers executing civil engineering projects. We help shape projects that are structurally sound, compliant and optimised for lifecycle performance.

Our teams integrate early – during concept and feasibility – and stay engaged through detailed design, tendering, site execution and handover as required.

Typical project types

  • ✓ Educational campuses and institutional infrastructure.
  • ✓ Residential townships, high-rise apartments and villa communities.
  • ✓ Commercial, office and mixed-use developments.

Who it is for

Owners, institutions & developers

Civil Engineering & Construction services are relevant if you:

  • ✓ Are planning to develop your own land and need a technically-strong partner.
  • ✓ Are an institution or NGO commissioning social infrastructure.
  • ✓ Are a developer seeking an independent project management and quality control layer.

Service breakdown

End-to-end technical consulting & construction support.

Engagements are tailored to your project’s stage and internal capabilities. We can either anchor the full lifecycle or step in for specific stages.

01 · Early stage

Feasibility & planning

  • • Site assessment and constraints analysis.
  • • Best-use options studies and concept briefs.
  • • Preliminary cost and time estimates.

02 · Design & approvals

Detailed Project Reports

  • • Technical due diligence and validation of design proposals.
  • • Detailed Project Reports (DPR) for stakeholders and lenders.
  • • Coordination with architects, structural and MEP consultants.

03 · Execution

Construction management

  • • Contractor evaluation, tendering support and negotiation inputs.
  • • Site supervision, quality checks and progress monitoring.
  • • Lender’s Engineer role and reporting, where required.
